Our Process

Get a quote

Below is the quotation form we allow all our customers to partake in. It is the best way to see if our prices fit into your budgets, also to compare prices.

Contact

Contact us on what service you are in need of. We will then refer you to one of our consultants.

Consultation

We now need you to in detail give an explaination of what service you need ,how big of a job it is , if it is a emergency repair.If it is a from scratch construction job then we will need to refer you the the available Architect. If it is a repair job we will refer you to an available employee.

The Plans and Design approvals

You will receive all the plans and contract agreements going forward from your Architect and for others Interior designs , you can go through it and seek legal advice from your personal attorney.We will wait for your approval and the signed contract agreement before we can start.

Hire

You can now decide if you will be hiring the employee we connected you with. A deposit according to the quotation will now be needed for the employees to start work.

One on One communication

You would now be given contact details of the person who will provide you with any one of our services. The clients in need of long term projects will now be in contact with Architects who will need to ask some questions to find your style, your must haves in the home or building and what layout you will be comfortable with.
